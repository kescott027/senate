# BILL-2026-03-15-AM-LAW-009-INTENT-HIL

## Summary
Amend LAW-009 to require explicit human-in-the-loop approval for any intent changes during execution.

## Proposed Amendment

Add to LAW-009: AI Workflows

### New Section: Intent Change Gate
- Any change to the declared project intent or scope requires HIL approval.
- The approval must be logged with rationale and linked to the relevant sprint or backlog change.

## Rationale
Prevents unapproved scope drift and preserves alignment with stakeholder intent.

## Impact
Adds an explicit approval step for scope/intent changes.

## Vote Recommendation
Approve to strengthen intent alignment in AI workflows.
